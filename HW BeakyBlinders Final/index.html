How to Adopt




Step 1

To ensure that you are prepared to take on the challenges- and experience the joys- of sharing your life with a parrot, we require all potential adopters to fill up the adoption form.

Step 2

To help break the cycle of bird acquisition and rehoming, we require all adopters to attend our education programing to set them up with the skills and knowledge to live with these amazing creatures.

Step 3

The final step is to schedule a home visit with our Adoption Coordinators. The purpose of this home visit is to ensure your home is properly suited to house a parrot.
