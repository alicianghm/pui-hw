How to Adopt


Step 1

To ensure that you are prepared to take on the challenges, we require all potential adopters to fill up the adoption form.

Step 2

We require all adopters to attend our education program to pick up skills and knowledge to live with these amazing pets.

Step 3

The final step is to schedule a home visit with our Adoption Coordinators to ensure your home is properly suited to house a parrot.
